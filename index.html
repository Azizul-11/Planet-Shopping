Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun. With a diameter of about 4,879 kilometers (3,265 miles),
it is the smallest planet in our solar system by diameter, not by mass. Mercury is the only planet known to have a solid surface, making it the only known planet to have a solid surface that is not a water ice body.
